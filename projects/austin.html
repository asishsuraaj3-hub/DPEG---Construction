Austin Residential & Mixed-Use

Austin, TX 200,000 sq ft Completed 2024

This 200,000 sq ft development in Austin combines single-family residential with mixed-use retail. The project showcases DPEG's end-to-end capability—from concept to keys—with one team and full accountability.

Challenge

Complex site with multiple product types and a demanding urban infill schedule. The owner wanted a single point of contact for all construction activities.

Solution

Our integrated team managed site development, residential construction, and retail build-out under one contract. Weekly coordination meetings and a shared project management platform kept everyone aligned.

Results

  • Delivered on schedule
  • Single point of accountability simplified owner communication
  • Safety record: zero incidents
