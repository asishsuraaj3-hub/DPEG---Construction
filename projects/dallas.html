Dallas Retail & Shopping Center

Dallas, TX 85,000 sq ft Completed 2023

This 85,000 sq ft retail center in Dallas demonstrates DPEG's capability in commercial development. The project included anchor tenant build-out, pad sites, and shared infrastructure.

Challenge

Multiple tenant delivery dates and phased occupancy required careful coordination between shell construction and tenant improvements.

Solution

We established a master schedule with clear milestones for each tenant. Our team managed both shell and TI work, ensuring each tenant received their space on time.

Results

  • All tenants delivered on schedule
  • Grand opening on target date
  • Strong post-construction relationship for future work
