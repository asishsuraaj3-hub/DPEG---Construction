Houston Multi-Family Development

Houston, TX 125,000 sq ft Completed 2024

This 125,000 sq ft multi-family development in Houston represents DPEG Construction's commitment to scalable communities built on time and on budget. The project included 312 units across three buildings with shared amenities, pool, and clubhouse.

Challenge

Tight timeline and supply chain constraints required proactive procurement and clear scheduling. The client needed delivery before Q4 2024 lease-up targets.

Solution

Our team implemented early material ordering, phased construction sequencing, and weekly owner updates. We delivered two weeks ahead of schedule and under budget.

Results

  • Delivered 2 weeks ahead of schedule
  • Under budget by 3%
  • Zero lost-time safety incidents
